About Peter Rabbit

Peter Rabbit is a brave little bunny adventuring everyday. He has a family called the Rabbit family. Those are: Grey Rabbit(Dad), Peach Rabbit(Mum), Peter Rabbit, White Rabbit(Sister), and Peter Bad. Peter Bad does not look anything like a rabbit. Instead, he is a fox that is fast and sly, causing trouble EVERYDAY.

So in order to destroy Peter Bad, he needs to find a hidden switch in his house. Peter Rabbit's house is actually a trampoline. This will make it harder because the walls are see through. When someone turns off the switch, Peter Bad will be no more :D
